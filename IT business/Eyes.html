Cataracts

Early Symptoms : None.

Later Symptoms : Blurry vision, colors that seem faded, sensitivity to light, trouble seeing at night, double vision.

Diagnosis : Dilated eye exam.

Treatment : Surgery.

Dry Eyes

Symptoms : Burning, dry or scratchy feeling, blurry vision, red eyes.

Diagnosis : Dilated eye exam, measuring amount and thickness of tears.

Treatment : Medicine(eye drops) lifestyle changes.

AMD

Early Symptoms: None.

Later Symptoms : Loss of the central vision you need to see details.

Diagnosis : Dilated eye exam.

Treatment : Dietary supplements,injections,photodynamic therapy.

Amblyopia (Lazy Eye)

Symptoms : Poor Vision in 1 eye.

Diagnosis : Eye exam.

Treatment : Eye drop or wearing an eye patch.

Astigmatism

Symptoms : Headaches, blurry vision, eye strain, trouble seeing at night.

Diagnosis : Dilated eye exam.

Treatment : Eyeglasses, contact lenses, surgery.

Color Blindness

Symptoms : Trouble telling the differences between colors.

Diagnosis : Color vision test.

Treatment : Special glasses and contacts, visual aids.

Diabetic Retinopathy

Early Symptoms : None.

Later Symptoms : Blurry vison, floating spots in your vision, blindness.

Later Symptoms : Blurry vison, floating spots in your vision, blindness.

Diagnosis : Dilated eye exam.

Treatment : Injections, laser treatment , surgery.

Floaters

Symptoms : Small dark sports or squlggy lines that float across your vision.

Diagnosis : Dilated eye exam.

Treatment : None(in most cases), surgery.

Glaucoma

Early Symptoms : Often none.

Later Symptoms : Loss of side (peripheral) vision, blind spots, blindness.

Diagnosis : Dilated eye exam with visual field testing.

Treatment : Medicine (eye drops), laser treatment,surgery.

Retinal Detachment

Symptoms : A sudden increases in floaters, flashes of light in one eye or both, a "curtain" or shadow over your field of vision.

Diagnosis : Dilated eye exam.

Treatment : Laser treatment, surgery, must go to eye doctor.

Retinal detachment can cause permanent vision loss.
